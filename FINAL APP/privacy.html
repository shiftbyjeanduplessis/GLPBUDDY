Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

GLP Buddy stores account details, onboarding information, app usage data, check-ins, weight logs, workout activity, meal preferences, and progress photos so the app can function properly and improve over time.

What we collect

  • Account details such as email address
  • Profile and onboarding information
  • Weight logs, daily check-ins, symptoms, hydration, workouts, and meal-plan usage
  • Progress photos you upload
  • Product analytics such as page views and feature usage

How we use information

  • To provide app functionality and save your progress
  • To improve the product and understand usage during testing
  • To troubleshoot support issues
  • To create aggregated product reports and pitch materials that do not identify you personally

Progress photos

Progress photos are treated as private user content. They are stored to support your progress tracking and are not shared publicly by default.

Data access

Your data should only be visible to you unless you later explicitly join a coach or clinician workflow, or unless access is required for product support and maintenance.

Your choices

You can request account deletion and ask questions about your stored data through the support channel provided by the product owner.
