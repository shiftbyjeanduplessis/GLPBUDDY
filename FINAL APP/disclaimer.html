Medical and Coaching Disclaimer

Last updated: March 15, 2026

GLP Buddy is an educational and behavior-support tool. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease, and it does not replace medical care.

  • Any targets or meal structures should be reviewed in context of your own health needs.
  • Medication use, side effects, and symptom concerns should be discussed with a healthcare professional.
  • If you feel unwell or have urgent medical concerns, seek direct medical care rather than relying on the app.

Any future coach or clinician dashboards are intended to support oversight, not replace proper clinical judgment.
